Enroll for our B.Sc. Aviation Course today!

Voyage Bsc. in Aviation is a three years degree course affiliated to the University of Bangalore. For students who have an Indian CPL, admission can be sought out directly in second year. Also called as Lateral entry according to the guidelines of University of Bangalore.

REQUIREMENTS

All persons who have attained the age of seventeen (17) years and who have passed the Higher Secondary School Certificate Examination (HSC). A Pass in Physics and Mathematics is Mandatory. A Candidate is expected to be fluent in English. A candidate is required to successfully carry out Class II MEDICAL EXAMINATION before commencing flying training. As per DGCA rules Police verification of selected Candidates will be carried out and Voyage will facilitate early processing of applications for this purpose.
