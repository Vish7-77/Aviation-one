From The Principal's Desk

As pilots progress in their careers, they acquire skills which are highly specialized and technical in nature. However, there was an absence of a suitable University Degree Course to reflect their professional qualifications.

To provide pilots with a conventional academic platform equal to their skills and knowledge, the Federation of Indian Pilots founded the Institute of Aviation and Aviation Safety, in 2002 with affiliation to the venerable University of Bangalore for awarding B.Sc. (Aviation) Degrees.

A formal Degree broadens a pilot’s perspective and opens the portals to Post Graduation leading to greater professional excellence. Therefore it is important to select an academy that provides an experienced faculty and a structured curriculum. IAAS is the 1st Institute in Bangalore to be affiliated to the University of bangalore and has achieved many milestones in the last 8 years of imparting quality training to its students. Voyage carries out Flying Training of its students in the best flying academies in Canada. The flying clubs are also committed to use most modern and effective training systems, faculty and best global practices to impart safe and comprehensive training for professional pilots in the defined duration. The students undergo 200hrs of Flying including Multi Engine Rating and Instrument Rating.
