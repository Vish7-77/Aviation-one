FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

All your questions about courses and admissions answered here.

What courses does VOYAGE offer ?

BSc. in Aviation is the only course we are offering right now. We are in the process of adding
new courses. You may watch this space for more information.

What are the basic requirements to enroll for the B.Sc. in Aviation Course ?

Any student who has passed HSC (A Levels) or equivalent with Physics, Math and English
may apply for the B.Sc. Aviation course.

Is the B.Sc. Aviation course accredited by any University?

Yes. VOYAGE is affiliated to the University of Bangalore. Bsc. in Aviation is conducted according
to the precedence set for colleges by the University of Bangalore.

Is there any concession applicable to serving commercial pilots ?

Indian CPL/ATPL holders regardless of their job situation may apply for admission directly
to the Second Year of B.Sc. Aviation.

Does the B.Sc. Aviation Course involve flying training ?

Flying training is compulsory for procuring final credits and marksheet from the University.
Students will be required to complete 200hrs of flying and have a valid Commercial Pilots
License for procuring the marksheet. Non-adherence to these terms allows the University to
withhold the final marksheet and not grant the degree.

Does the Bsc. in Aviation course from VOYAGE provide flying training ?

No

Does the Bsc. in Aviation course from VOYAGE recommend flying training institutes in India or Abroad ?

No

Does VOYAGE provide accommodation for outstation students ?

No

What are the fees ?

Fees for Bsc. in Aviation are Rs. 66100/- per year

Can the fee payment be made in installments ?

In lieu of covid-19 we college has granted permission to pay fees in two installments; First
installment Rs. 33100 and Second installment Rs. 33000/- The payment has to be made at the
beginning of each semester.

Is BSc. in Aviation a correspondence course ?

No. BSc. in Aviation is a full-time degree course affiliated to the University of Bangalore. All
guidelines to be followed are those of the University. For any other queries related to
attendance kindly reach out to the Administrative Staff.

Can I enroll for a BSc. in Aviation course, online ?

With our new website, VOYAGE has enabled admissions purely online. You may request a
prospectus online by making a nominal prospectus fee of Rs. 600/-
You may then fill out the form and pay your first year fees online through our portal or via
NEFT.

Will I need to go through a police verification ?

The DGCA requires all aviation degree candidates to go through a Police Verification. VOYAGE
will assist a candidate in the same.

Do you have a Masters in Aviation Program ?

VOYAGE is currently in the process of gaining approval from the University of Bangalore for its
M.Sc. in Aviation course. You may watch this space for more information.
